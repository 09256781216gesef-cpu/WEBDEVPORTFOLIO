ABOUT

Darelle Daymiel

Dar is a current third-year MMA student and a freelance creative specializing in photography, design, and illustration—but now is delving into web development and design. Her works are inspired by many different genres of photography and graphic design, with her illustrations being stylized and simple. She incorporates her interests in her works, such as motorsports, street photography, animation, and music.

Gesef Depra

Gesefaaa is an independent freelance digital artist who curates custom digital artworks such as illustrations, portraits, concept art, character design or graphic assets. Her target audience mainly consists of people in art communities or spaces where original ideas thrive such as Dungeons and Dragons, RWBY and Anime enjoyers. Other than these, Gesefaaa also gets clients who need digital assets such as streamers and indie game developers.

Lyra Rejaba

Lyra is an MMA college student who works freelance by taking commissions as a digital artists. Her art is mainly about whimsiness and being experimental with colors. She incorporates fantasy elements into her art, mainly inspired from songs, sceneries, or overall character and world-building..
